The product

Meet your company brain.

It connects your whole stack: docs, email, CRM, code. It learns how your business runs. We build the integrations around your workflows.

Integrations

Built around your stack

We wire it into the exact tools your team already uses. Slack, Notion, Gmail, your CRM, your repos. No new login, no platform to learn. It shows up where the work already happens.

Knowledge

One source of truth

Every doc, thread and ticket, instantly findable. The brain reads your stack so your team stops digging through tabs to answer the same question twice. Ask once, get the answer the senior would give.

Automation

Busywork on autopilot

Notes, follow-ups and handoffs run themselves. The brain spots what needs to happen next and drafts it, files it, or pings the right person. You only step in when you actually need to.

Onboarding

Onboard new staff in days

New hires ask the brain instead of the whole team. Same answer the senior would give, in seconds, with the context already loaded. Day one feels like month three.

Interface

Just talk to it

If they can write a message, they can use it. No prompt engineering, no syntax. Talk to it the way you'd brief a colleague, and it asks back when it's unsure.

Security

Yours by default

You control what it sees and what stays local. Connections are off by default, switched on source by source, with a full audit trail. Your data never trains an outside model.

Privacy & control

Your data never leaves your control.

Every file stays local by default. You decide, source by source, what the brain can see and touch.

  • Files stay private unless you connect them
  • Turn any connection on or off, any time
  • We never train outside models on your data
